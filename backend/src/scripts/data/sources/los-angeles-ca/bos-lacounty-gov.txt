The Board of Supervisors is the governing body of the County of Los Angeles, a charter county. As such, it has the unique function of serving as the executive and legislative head of the largest and most complex county government in the entire United States. A civil service staff which performs the duties for the County departments and agencies serves the needs of the County's population of more than 10 million people. Learn More Hilda L. Learn More Office of Inspector General The OIG provides independent and comprehensive oversight, monitoring of, and reporting about the Sheriff's and Probation Department.
